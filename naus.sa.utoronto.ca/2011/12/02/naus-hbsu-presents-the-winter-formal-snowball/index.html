NAUS & HBSU Presents the Winter Formal: Snowball!

Hey guys!

For the first time ever, the Human Biology Students’ Union (HBSU) and the Neuroscience Association for Undergraduate Students (NAUS) are teaming up to host a Winter Formal for Human Biology students.

Snowball! A Winter Formal!
Friday, January 20, 2012
6:00 pm – 1:00 am
Hart House Great Hall

Tickets will be sold for $50, which includes a 3 course gourmet meal (vegan option available!), wine with dinner, 2 drink tickets, and an amazing night! For how to buy, see below.

Early ticket-buyers will have the advantage of organizing their own table seating, so buy your tickets soon! After dinner, we will party the night away in class with an amazing DJ and offer some surprise late night treats, so we guarantee there will be something there for everyone!

Most importantly, you are welcome to bring anyone you like, even if they aren’t in any Human Biology or Neuroscience! Just remember to buy your tickets early! So mark this date down on your calendar, it’s going to be a great event! Whether you’re in first or second year looking to meet new people, in third year looking to escape the library, in fourth or fifth year ready to celebrate your upcoming convocation or an alumnus, join us for a classy, dashing time.

Do not hesitate to contact us with any questions you may have via this facebook event, which contains the latest updates on its wall. Alternatively, you can contact us at either naus@utoronto.ca or hbsu.sa@utoronto.ca.

**** Ticket Sales ****

Regular sales are over due to exams, but some extra sales times have been arranged for next week due to high demand:

M/T/R/F, December 12/13/15/16, 2011
12 pm – 3 pm
HBSU Office (where?)

We are also aiming to employ an Interac e-mail money transfer system for ticket sales over the holidays for those still interested in buying tickets, though it might cost a few dollars extra due to service fees. We will sell any tickets that remain the first week we return to school in January. Please don’t delay buying your tickets! It will be a great event and we don’t want anyone missing out!

NAUS Exec
